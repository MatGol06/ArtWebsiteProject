# 🎨 The Art Gallery of You

> *Every artist has a masterpiece. This is mine.*

An interactive storytelling website built with React that transforms a simple confession into a digital art gallery experience. Inspired by modern portfolio websites and immersive web animations, the project guides visitors through a series of gallery rooms where each artwork reveals another chapter of the story.

---

## ✨ Overview

**The Art Gallery of You** is a frontend-focused project that combines modern UI/UX, smooth animations, and emotional storytelling.

Instead of displaying a traditional love letter, visitors explore a virtual gallery where every section represents an artwork, memory, or emotion before reaching the final confession.

The experience is designed to feel elegant, calm, and personal.

# 🖼 Gallery Rooms

## Room 01 — The First Sketch

The beginning of the story.

A pencil sketch animation introduces the first meeting and the idea that every masterpiece starts with a single line.

---

## Room 02 — The Colors

The gallery slowly fills with color.

Each section represents qualities admired in the person.

---

## Room 03 — Inspiration

A gallery showcasing moments, memories, and emotions.

Inspired by sketchbooks, mood boards, and design concepts.

---

## Room 04 — The Letter

A digital sketchbook opens.

The visitor flips through pages before reaching a handwritten letter.

---

## Room 05 — The Confession

The final artwork remains covered until the visitor reveals it.

The exhibition concludes with the confession.
